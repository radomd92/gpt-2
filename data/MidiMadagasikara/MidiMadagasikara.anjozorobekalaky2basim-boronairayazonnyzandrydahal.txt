ANJOZOROBE : KALAKY 2, BASIM-BORONA IRAY AZON’NY ZANDRY, DAHALO 4 MATY – MIDI MADAGASIKARA

niseho tany amin’ny faritra Anjozorobe. Vokany, dahalo 3 sy olona iray no namoy ny ainy. Basy mahery 
kalky roa, basim-borona iray ary bala miisa 12 no azoan’ny zandary.
