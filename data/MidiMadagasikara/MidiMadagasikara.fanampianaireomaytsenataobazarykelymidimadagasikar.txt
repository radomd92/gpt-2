FANAMPIANA IREO MAY TSENA TAO BAZARY KELY – MIDI MADAGASIKARA

“Tsy marina ny filazana fa olona nahandro tamin’ireo mpivarotra hani-masaka no niavian’ny afo 
ireo fivarotana 41 teto Bazary kely. Aminay dia tena niniana nodorana ilay izy satria fivarotana 
nisy olona sy mpivarotra koveta ary fitaovana plastika no niavian’ny afo…”, izay no nambaran’ireto 
may fananana nandritra ity hain-trano nitranga tao Bazary kely ity, ny zoma 2 desambra alina lasa 
tokony tamin’ny 11ora. Efa samy nanarina ny tsenany may ireo mpivarotra samihafa ankehitriny 
efa miroso amin’ny varotra fanaony an-davanandro. Tsy nanadino azy ireo ny minisitra Ndahimananjara 
raha nahare ny fangatahan’izy ireo ka nanolotra fanitso sy vola tao anaty valopy ho azy ireo, omaly 
misandratr’andro.
