Manadio tena ny zandary 

Ora maromaro taty aoriana rehefa niparitaka tamin’ny tambajotra sosialy “Facebook” ity raharaha 
dia namoaka fanambarana ny eo anivon’ny “infocom seg” fa tovolahy roa nanao asan-jiolahy no lavo. 

Raha ny filazan’io fanambarana teo anivon’ny zandary io dia milaza fa “ asan-jiolahy mitam-piadiana 
no niseho tao Ambalakisoa Toamasina”. 

Sendra nandalo tampoka hono ireo zandary nanao fisafoana ka niditra an-tsehatra avy hatrany. 

Taiza tompoko no niavian’ireo basy vita gasy “ sy coupe- coupe” ? 

Tamin’ity volana janoary ity dia maromaro no fihetsika mamoehitra nataon’ny zandarimariam-pirenena

efatra.
