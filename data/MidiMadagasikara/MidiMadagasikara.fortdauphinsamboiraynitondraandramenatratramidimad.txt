ny mbola fitrandrahana sy fanondranana ity hazo sarobidy ity any amin’ny tapany avaratry ny distrika 
ity. Notazonina ao an-dadoany na port teo aloha ity sambo iray ity, amin’izao fotoana. Mandeha 
fanadihadiana mahakasika ilay sambo sy ireo olona voarohirohy. Mahakasika ilay sambo rendrika
