iray 20 taona ity ramatoa 26 taona.Teo am-pandriana mihitsy izy ireo no sarona ka tsy afaka nihetsika

hidim-baravarana ilay bandy bôgôsy mpangala-badin’olona, ka naratra mafy vaky loha. Matin’ny 
sy daroka ihany koa ramatoa mpanitsakitsa-bady. Folaka hatramin’ny tanany. Mpamily no asan’ity 
tompom-bady ity ary samy manana lakilé hidin’ny trano izy mivady. Nody tampoka tany an-tranony
