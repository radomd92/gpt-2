Tsy fisian’ny asa, fahantrana, zaza ratsy taiza? Tovolahy tsy ampy taona telo, 16 taona, tratra 
am-panendahana voasambotry ny polisin’ny boriborintany fahatelo, teny Ankadivato, ny herinandro 
Tokony ho tamin’ny 6 ora sy sasany hariva, finday tany amin’ny olona hamonjy fodiana no nendahin’izy 
Niantso vonjy io. Nisy polisy tsy lavitra teo ka raikitra ny fisamborana. Tsy lasa lavitra, tratra 
telo lahy kely. Rehefa nofotorana ireo, nilaza fa nohon’ny tsy fananam-bola hidirana amina “balle 
jeunes” eny an-toerana no antony hanendahan’izy ireo. 

Tsy zava-baovao eny Ankadivato sy ny manodidina iny, anefa ny fanendahana rehefa hariva. Hatrany
