ka izay vao raikitra ny ady sy ny fifamonoana . Tsy nahazaka anefa ity farany ka avy hatrany dia nandray 
mba hamelezana ity rangahy . Tsy noraharahainy na dia efa zokiolona sady rafozany aza ity rangahy 
fa dia novelesiny avy hatrany . Tsy nahatsiaro tena intsony ity rangahy antitra ity rehefa avy novelesina
