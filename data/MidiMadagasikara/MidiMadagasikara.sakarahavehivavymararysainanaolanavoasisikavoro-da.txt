SAKARAHA : VEHIVAVY MARARY SAINA NAOLANA, VOASISIKA VORO-DAMBA NY FIVAVIANY – MIDI MADAGASIKARA

Noho ny filan-dratsy mamoehatra amin’ny fahafinaretana ara-nofo, dia efa lasa mihabiby ny olona 
Vehivavy iray manodidina ny 40 taona eo, voalaza fa marary saina, no hitan’ny olona niampatra vao 
tokony ho tamin’ny 5 ora sy sasany, teo amoron-dalana naratra mafy tsy nahatsiaro tena, omaly, 
Sakaraha. Araka ny tati-baovao voaray, dia naolana sady nodarohana ho faty ity ramatoa adala mirenireny 
ary nosisihan’ny tsy mataho-tody voro-damba ny fivaviany. Rehefa noheverin’ny nanolana sy namono 
fa maty ity vehivavy ity, dia natsipiny tao anaty lavaka.
