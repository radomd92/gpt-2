rongony, nirongo antsy, tao Ampasikibo Toliara, tamin’ny toerana somary mangina sady maizina, 
hariva. Raha ny fanazavana azo dia niezaka nanohitra sy niaro tena ilay tovolahy fa lasa ihany ny 
teny an-tanany sady voatsatoka antsy izy. Nitsoaka nandositra kosa ilay tovovavy sady niantso

tamin’ny olona maromaro, saingy ilay tovolahy efa nihosin-dra no sisa teo. Mbola faritra mena
