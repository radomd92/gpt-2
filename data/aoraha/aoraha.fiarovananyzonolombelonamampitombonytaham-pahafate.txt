amintsika. Tsy mbola voalaza ao anatin’izany ireo any ambanivohitra tsy nanaovana fanadihadiana. 
an’ireo firenena efa manome alalana malalaka io fomba fanao iray io anefa dia efa nihena 91% izany”,
