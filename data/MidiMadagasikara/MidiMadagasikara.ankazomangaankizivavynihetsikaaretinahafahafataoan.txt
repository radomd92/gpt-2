ANKAZOMANGA : ANKIZIVAVY NIHETSIKA ARETINA HAFAHAFA TAO ANATY TAKSIBE – MIDI MADAGASIKARA

Ankizivavy sahabo ho 13 taona avy nianatra handeha hody no nihetsika aretina hafahafa toa ambalavelona 
anaty taksibe, omaly antoandro tokony ho tamin’ny 12 ora mahery kely teo, tetsy Ankazomanga. Izy 
nihizitrizitra sy nikiakiana ary nifotafota ivy (faroratra fotsy teo amin’ny vavany) no nanaitra 
rehetra tao anatin’ity fiara fitateram-bahoaka ity. Voatery nijanona mihitsy ilay bus, satria 
ireo mpandeha. Niditra teo Antanimena ambany ity zazavavy ity sy namany iray, ary raha vao tafapetraka 
dia nanohy nijery facebook tamin’ny findainy, hoy ny fanazavan’ny mpandeha iray teo akaikiny, 
tampoka teo nikiakiaka izy raha nahita sarin-dehilahy tao anaty tambajotran-tserasera. Niantsoantso 
iray ity tovovavy mpianatra ity, avy eo nihijitra. Dimy minitra taorian’io, nisy niantso azy tamin’ny 
ary nisava teo ilay fihetsehany aretina hafahafa nihatra taminy. Nidina teo 67 ha Avaratra Atsinanana 
tovovavy ity sy ilay namany.
