no nahavoatifitra ity dahalo raindahiny ity. Tafaporitsaka kosa ny namany. Avy nangalatra omby 
200 tao an-toerana ireto olon-dratsy ireto no nifanehatra tamin’ny zandary. Mitohy ny fanarahan-dia 
dahalo. Tsy mandry fahalemana iny lalana mihazo an’i Taolagnaro manomboka eo an-tampon’Ihorombe 
an’i Betroka
