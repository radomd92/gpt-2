Nandositra, taorian’izay, ilay voarohirohy. Tsy mbola hita izy, araka ny vaovao farany, omaly. 

Noenti-katerezana ny fianakavian’ity tovolahy maty voadaroka. Nanapa-kevitra ny hanatona

niaraka nanao rodobe nitondra basim-borona sy antsy fa hoe hanao valifaty.
